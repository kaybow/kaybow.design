CYCLE SECURE APP

IOT PRODUCT UX/UI DESIGN

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Cycle Secure is an IoT bicycle locking and security mobile application paired with a GPS location device that attaches to the bike itself. The Cycle Secure device is fitted with both a motion and vibration sensor that notifies the owner when their lock is being tampered with or if their bike is on the move.

CLIENT: ALGONQUIN COLLEGE OF APPLIED ARTS AND TECHNOLOGY
PROJECT DURATION:2 WEEKS

OPPORTUNITY

With environmental concerns on the rise, more and more people are opting to use a bicycle as their main mode of transportation. Unfortunately, as many as half of all active cyclists have had their bike stolen at some point in their lifetime, and few are ever recovered. A combination security and tracking system would be a valuable asset to any cyclist.
