ABOUT

My Story

My journey into the graphic design industry has been an evolutionary experience: A childhood joy of colouring books and macaroni art turned into a love of paint and mixed media in my adolescence.

I studied fine arts but was intrigued by the wonderful, intricate world of graphic design.

Design Focus

Refusing to retire my beloved brushes and paints for a Macbook and Adobe software, I aim to fuse my two passions together to create dynamic solutions to design problems.

While I consider myself cross-disciplinary, I specialize in [and most enjoy] illustration, branding, photography and UX/UI design.

Things I Love

Having been raised in a tiny town in the Ottawa Valley, I grew up with a fondness and appreciation of the great outdoors. I love exploring new and unfamiliar places, going for hikes, and skiing the slopes in the winter.

When I'm stuck indoors, I can be found drinking coffee and making oil and mixed media experiments [messes] in my studio.
