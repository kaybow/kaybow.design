CHORGANIZER APP

UX/UI DESIGN

PROJECT OVERVIEW

A problem-solving iOS mobile application design project; Chorganizer came to fruition after following a set flow of research and development: 1) Research and Discovery 2) Identifying the Target Audience and User's Needs 3) Flowcharts and Storyboarding 4) Prototyping & Usability Testing and 5) Art Direction

CLIENT: ALGONQUIN COLLEGE OF APPLIED ARTS AND TECHNOLOGY
PROJECT DURATION: 8 WEEKS

RESEARCH AND TARGET AUDIENCE

After interviewing students living with roommates, and identifying their needs and frustrations, a problem statement was created: “User needs a way to communicate with roommates to share the load of household chores. Interestingly, user rarely interacts with roommate(s) and is frustrated with the lack of communication that leads to a disorganized household.”

DEFINE AND IDEATE

Chorganizer is an organizational application for aiding roommates in sharing household chores and responsibilities and is targeted directly toward college-age roommates living together.

Requiring group participation and interactivity, the app focuses solely on chore completion to keep usability simple and straightforward. Unlike most chore apps, Chorganizer's points reward system is included in the app and not as an add-on feature. As a bonus, Chorganizer will be available for use across all platforms and can be synced to various devices the user(s) may have.
