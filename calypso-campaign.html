CALYPSO WATERPARK

-MULTI-PLATFORM ADVERTISING CAMPAIGN-

PROJECT OVERVIEW

The Calypso Theme Waterpark campaign was an exercise in designing for various advertising platforms: web banners, print advertising, environmental signage and social media, while keeping a cohesive and engaging theme.

The theme of the assignment was to entice people to purchase season summer passes to the waterpark.

CLIENT: ALGONQUIN COLLEGE OF APPLIED ARTS AND TECHNOLOGY
PROJECT DURATION: 4 WEEKS

CHALLENGE

The primary challenge of this campaign was to speak to the broad age range of people who typically purchase passes or tickets to Calypso Water Park.

The secondary challenge was to echo a single theme across various digital and print mediums.

RESEARCH

Through my research, I discovered that Calypso’s main target audience is families of an age range of 7-45 years of age. Visitors to the waterpark are often parents with young children who respond well to advertisements that utilize a bright colour palette and fun themes. Calypso’s secondary target audience are groups of pre-teens and teenagers aged 12-19 years of age.

CONCEPT AND STRATEGY

The tone of sarcastic humour paired with a bright summer colour palette was used to communicate to Calypso’s target audience with a broad age range of approximately 7-45.

Characters of various ages were developed and portrayed attempting to cool off in various humorous ways—a middle-aged man sitting in a kiddie pool, a young girl with a melting ice-cream cone pressed to her forehead and a teenage boy holding a hand-held mini fan to the side of his face. The illustrated characters are captioned with a sarcastic “REALLY?”, asking the audience, is this really how you want to stay cool in the summer heat?

FINAL DESIGN

To represent the generations-old family farm theme, the typewriter-style typeface has a weathered, distressed appearance. An egg with the letter "B", for "Beking's" is centered within a textured nest, clearly representing the family's primary product; farm-fresh eggs.
